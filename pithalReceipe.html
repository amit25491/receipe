Pithal-Bhakar Receipe

Ingredients:

1. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
2. 8 cloves garlic
3. 1 cup gram flour (besan)
4. 2 inches ginger
5. 8 pieces green chillies
6. salt as required
7. 6 cup water
8. 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida
9. 1 teaspoon turmeric
10. 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
11. 2 tablespoon refined oil

Instruction:

Step 1 Chop onions & prepare ginger-garlic-green chillies paste
To start with, take a chopping board and chop the onions. Now in a blender jar add ginger, green chillies and garlic cloves. Blend until you obtain a smooth paste.

Step 2 Saute the onion
Now in a deep shallow pan, heat oil over low flame. To this, add asafoetida, mustard seeds and turmeric. Once the seeds start to pop up, put onion pieces. Saute them until the colour changes to light brown.

Step 3 Add ginger-garlic-green chilli paste, salt & water
Now mix in the prepared paste and saute. Now pour water and season it with salt. Stir nicely and bring the water to boil.

Step 4 Stir in the gram flour until desired consistency is achieved
Once the water reaches a boiling point, mix in gram flour and stir frequently until the curry is not too thin or thick in consistency. Serve Maharashtrian pithla bhakri hot!
