Kande-Pohe Receipe

Ingredients:

1. 1 and 1/2 cup of Poha / Jada poha / Thick Poha
2. 2 medium size onions finely chopped / Kanda
3. 1 small potato or 1/3 cup cubed and boiled potato / Aloo
4. 2-3 green chillies / Hari Mirch
5. 3 tblsp of roasted peanuts / Shengdana
6. 2 tblsp Groundnut oil
7. 1/2 tsp Mustard seeds or Rai
8. 1/2 tsp Jeera or Cumin seeds
9. 1 or 2 sprigs of Curry leaves
10. 1/2 tsp of Turmeric powder
11. a pinch of Hing or asafoetida
12. 1 tsp sugar
13. 1 Lemon (extract juice / cut into wedges / pieces for garnish)
14. Salt to taste
15. Finely chopped coriander leaves as required
16. 1 or 2 tblsp of grated coconut as garnish while serving


INSTRUCTIONS

Prepare and keep aside your ingredients like chopped onions, boiled and cubed potatoes.
Take poha or flattened rice in a colander and rinse couple of times under running tap water, drain excess water and keep aside.
Heat oil in a pan or kadhai, add mustard seeds. Once they crackle, add Jeera or cumin seeds.
Once cumin turns slightly brown (roasted), add hing or asafoetida and roasted groundnuts. Saute them for a couple of seconds.
Now add curry leaves, green chillies, finely chopped onions, mix well and cook until the onions are translucent.
add the boiled and cubed potatoes and Turmeric powder. Mix well and cook for few seconds.
The washed and drained poha must have fluffed up well. Add salt to it and toss gently.
Now add the fluffed up poha to the kadhai or pan along with sugar and finely chopped coriander leaves.
Mix well and let it cook for a couple of minutes over low flame with lid on.
Switch off the flame, serve it piping hot in bowls or plates with a lime wedge, some grated coconut on top and some finely chopped coriander leaves.
f not serving for guests, then sqeeze the lemon juice in the kadhai itself (after switching off the flame) and then serve immediately.
