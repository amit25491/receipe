Vadapav Receipe

For Aloo Mixture:
1. 2 tsp oil
2. ½ tsp mustard
3. pinch of hing / asafoetida
4. few curry leaves
5. 1 inch ginger (crushed)
6. 2 clove garlic (crushed)
7. 1 chilli (finely chopped)
8. 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)
9. ¼ tsp turmeric / haldi
10. 2 potato / aloo (boiled & mashed)
11. ½ tsp salt
12. 1 tbsp lemon juice


For besan batter:
1. ¾ cup besan / gram flour
2. 1 tbsp rice flour
3. ¼ tsp turmeric / haldi
4. ¼ tsp kashmiri red chilli powder / lal mirch powder
5. pinch of hing / asafoetida
6. ¼ tsp salt
7. ¼ tsp baking soda
8. ½ cup water
9. oil for deep frying


Other ingredients:
1. 6 ladi pav / dinner rolls
2. 7 green chilli
3. 6 tsp green chutney
4. 3 tsp tamarind chutney
5. 3 tsp dry garlic chutney

INSTRUCTIONS

firstly, take ball sized aloo mixture and dip in besan batter and coat well.
deep fry in hot oil stirring occasionally.
now slit the ladi pav and spread 1 tsp green chutney, ½ tsp tamarind chutney and ½ tsp dry garlic chutney.
place the prepared vada in center of pav, chura and fried chilli.
finally, press the vada pav and serve immediately.
